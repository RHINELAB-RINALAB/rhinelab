abstract: >-
  Using the quantum kinetic equation method, this review investigates electron
  transport in low-dimensional systems under the simultaneous influence of a
  magnetic field, a static electric field, and a laser field. It derives an
  explicit analytical expression for the Ettingshausen coefficient and clarifies
  electron–optical-phonon scattering mechanisms. For GaAs/GaAsAl quantum wells the
  effect is enhanced relative to bulk semiconductors as a consequence of quantum
  confinement, with a non-linear dependence on temperature and laser frequency.
