A planned seminar from the MTAIL group introducing the lab's theoretical
modelling approach — the theoretical–empirical gap, statistical models, and the
coupling of modelling theory.

The MTAIL group is inherited from the Double Descent — Statistical Learning
Theory Research Group, which focused on the double-descent phenomenon in machine
learning and on analysing statistical learning frameworks back to Valiant's
PAC-learning theory.
